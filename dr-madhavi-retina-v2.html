What We Treat

Retina Services

Comprehensive, specialist-led treatment for all retinal and eye conditions — explained clearly, treated expertly.

🩺

Diabetic Retinopathy

The leading cause of preventable blindness in diabetics

Diabetic Retinopathy occurs when high blood sugar levels damage the tiny blood vessels in the retina. It progresses silently — often with no symptoms in early stages — making regular screening critically important for all diabetic patients.

Warning Symptoms

Blurred visionDark spots / floatersVision fluctuating with sugarDifficulty readingSudden vision loss

Treatment Options

Retinal Laser PhotocoagulationAnti-VEGF InjectionsIntravitreal SteroidsVitrectomy (advanced)
⚡

Retinal Detachment

A medical emergency requiring urgent treatment

Retinal detachment occurs when the retina separates from its blood supply. Without prompt treatment, the detached retina permanently loses function. Time is vision — seek immediate help at the first sign of symptoms.

Warning Symptoms

Sudden floatersFlashing lightsShadow across visionCurtain over visionDistorted vision

Treatment Options

Laser RetinopexyPneumatic RetinopexyScleral BucklingVitrectomy
🔬

Macular Diseases

AMD, Macular Hole & Central Serous Retinopathy (CSCR)

The macula is the central part of the retina responsible for sharp vision. Conditions like AMD, Macular Hole, and CSCR affect this vital zone and can significantly impact your quality of life.

Warning Symptoms

Distorted central visionDark blank spot at centreDifficulty readingStraight lines appear bent

Treatment Options

Anti-VEGF InjectionsPhotodynamic TherapyMacular Hole SurgeryMonitoring (CSCR)
💉

Intravitreal Injections

Anti-VEGF & steroid therapy for retinal conditions

Intravitreal injections deliver medication directly into the vitreous cavity, acting precisely at the retina. Performed in a sterile procedure room under strict aseptic protocols for safety and comfort.

Available Medications

Bevacizumab (Avastin)Ranibizumab (Lucentis)Aflibercept (Eylea)Triamcinolone / Ozurdex

Cataract Surgery

Phaco & SICS for clear, restored vision

Dr. Madhavi performs both Phacoemulsification (Phaco) — the gold standard micro-incision technique — and SICS, with careful selection of the right lens implant for each patient's lifestyle and vision needs.

Warning Symptoms

Cloudy / blurred visionGlare & halosFaded coloursDouble vision in one eye

Surgery Options

Phacoemulsification (Stitchless)SICSMonofocal IOLPremium Multifocal IOL
